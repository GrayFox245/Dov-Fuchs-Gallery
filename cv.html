Artistic CV

Dov Fuchs

Digital artist based in Haifa, Israel, working with staged imagination, self-portraiture, symbolic rooms, and psychological narrative.

Dov Fuchs publishes regularly in Living the Photo Artistic Life, the online magazine of the international AWAKE artist group directed by Sebastian Michaels. Over 90 works appeared in the magazine between 2019 and 2026. He was selected as Featured Artist in the February 2024 issue.

Over 190 photographs have been published in the 1X gallery, and over 50 have received Head Curator awards. Hundreds of landscape images were sold through his Shutterstock contributor account.

In January 2027, Dov Fuchs is scheduled to participate in an international residency program at Chiasso Perduto in Florence, Italy, hosted at Machiavelli's family palace.
