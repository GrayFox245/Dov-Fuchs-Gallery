Series

Selected Digital Art Series

The gallery presents a changing current exhibition and a library of related conceptual series.

  • Portraits of the Inner World

    A temporary focus wall of inner states, staged psychological rooms, self-encounters, and visual memory fragments.

  • Absent

    A series about disappearance, missing bodies, suspended presence, and the visual shape of what is no longer fully there.

  • Fictional Realities

    Images inspired by poems, novels, mythology, and imagined literary worlds where fiction becomes an inhabitable reality.

  • The Time Traveler's Diary

    A journey through impossible encounters with history, art, memory, invention, and the strange privilege of arriving too early or too late.

  • Echoes Behind the Mirror

    A series of reflections, doubles, thresholds, and unstable identities behind and inside the mirror world.

  • Fading Horizons

    A meditation on time, mortality, decline, duration, and the human attempt to read meaning against disappearance.
