About the artist

Dov Fuchs

A journey through imaginary rooms, altered self-portraits, narrative scenes, memory, anxiety, humor, and wonder.

Dov Fuchs is a digital artist based in Haifa, Israel. His work combines photography, digital manipulation, staged spaces, self-portraiture, symbolic rooms, and narrative imagination.

The virtual gallery is conceived as both exhibition space and authored environment: visitors move through current exhibitions, selected series, artist information, and a small office containing documents and contact details.

The project is designed as a serious digital art setting for viewers, artists, curators, writers, and thoughtful visitors who want to encounter the work through atmosphere as well as information.
